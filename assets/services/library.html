our library provides a quite and peaceful place for students to study and work on their projects or anything,the library building is located at the middle of the university in order to make it easily accessible to anyone even the not so experienced with the university buildings

The library is divided into 5 floors and each has its own purpose and main focus:

Underground Floor:

This is mainly used for parking of students and generally anyone who wants to use the library, its intended for those who are inside the library or any building close by, other people can use their perspective collage parking space

First Floor:

This floor houses the main library offices like the reception office, manager office, and also some Hall's which are used for meetings or any official occasions and can also be booked by students or outsiders if they have an event that is worthy.

Second Floor:

In this floor you'll find students study areas, and classes for discussion and also separate areas for individual studies

Third Floor

This is where students and their colleges and also lecturers come to chill and relax after a long session of studying, or a busy schedule of classes, the main purpose behind this floor is to give everyone an open space to do other things they cant do on other floors, there is a coffee shop and very comfortable place to clear your head

Fourth Floor

Shhhhh... this is the silent reading/studying area, in here you'll only find individual spots for only one student, this floor also has the largest collection of books in the whole library ranging from old history books to new genetics engineering books and covering most unviersity courses as well

As you can see our library is a very diverse where people can conduct big important meetings, discuss that project they have to finish soon, read interesting new books, or just chill and enjoy the company.
